Target Audience

Long Beach Chamber of Commerence target audience who are interested in business, economic development, networking, and professional developments. Those might include business owners and managers who are seeking to expand and network with other business professionals within the city of Long Beach. Our Chambers of Commerce provides the resources to help businesses succeed within the community.
