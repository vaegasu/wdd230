Clean Design

Mazda

mazdausa.com

Mazda's traditional menu are well-organized and easy to sift through. It's sophiscated and clean. Their website has a good use of white-space. The spacing around all the objects allows people who are viewing the page to stay calm when looking through their inventory.

Clean design focuses on the careful and precise position of the important elements throughout the website.

Rule of Thirds

Zara

zara.com

The rule of thirds is used in web design to create balance within the images or other elements of the landing page. The rule of thirds is a method of breaking up an image or design into different sections using columns and rows that form a grid.

Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Contrast is the relationship between two or more design elements whose dramatic differences are emphasized when both elements are shown together. Contrast can be created by a variation in sizing, shape, color, or other features.
