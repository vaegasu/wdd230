White Space &
Clean Design

  • Mazda

    • mazdausa.com

    Mazda's menu are well-organized and easy to sift through. It's sophiscated and clean. Their website has a good use of white-space. The spacing around all the objects allows viewers to easily find what they are looking for.

    Clean design focuses on the careful and precise position of the important elements throughout the website.

    Thirds

    PARC:
    Contrast

  • Apple

    • apple.com

    The bold rainbow of colors are a huge contrast. The overall positioning of the phones are done so in a way that actually encourages the viewers to keep scrolling. The Apple Watch with a black background geared just to draw them in, a subtle, but powerful glow emanating from the screen.

    Contrast is the relationship between two or more design elements whose dramatic differences are emphasized when both elements are shown together.
